AER

Basketball is a high intensity sport with its own specific requirements for the sports person to perform effectively. This is a niche market with very limited players (Nike, and Adidas) who cater to the unique requirements of the players based on their physical structure. This attributes to a very high cost gear.

This venture is an effort to address these issues of high lost, and low durability, thereby make the gear more accessible to the sports person encouraging many youngsters to join the sport.

Mind Map

Questionnaire Worksheet

I set out with the agenda of understanding the needs of my end users. As I started the journey it came to light that there are multiple people that influence the buying pattern. Here, I have classified the user base as “Active” and “Passive”. Keeping this in mind, the questions were framed to gather data on user behavior, the product they are using currently and understand their unique requirements.

Brand Iterations

Based on the users responses and the objectives of my venture, I explored and ideated on various features such as logo, brand name, etc. Here is a detailed overview of the same.

Brand Identity

I was hooked on to the name ”AER” which denotes a combination of the learnings, the features I would like to emphasis on in the product and the agility that comes with a sport like basketball. Basketball has many elements which come together to make it a wholesome game. The idea of combining various elements reflected well with the objective of my venture. Hence, I zeroed down on AER as the brand name and created identities which reflects the same.
